About Me

Hi there! I'm Juntian Zheng (郑钧天), a 1st-year PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Minchen Li. Previously, I was an undergraduate student at Tsinghua University, majoring in Computer Science and Technology at IIIS, Tsinghua University (a.k.a. Yao Class, directed by Prof. Andrew Chi-Chih Yao). I'm broadly interested in computer graphics, robotics, and artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on integrating physics-based simulation with AI, and their application in robot skill acquisition.

Started from 2022, I have been an undergraduate member at the 3DVICI Lab of IIIS, advised by Prof. Li Yi. During my research internship at MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab (2023.03 - 2023.08), I was advised by Prof. Chuang Gan and Prof. Josh Tenenbaum, working on differentiable physics simulation, physics-augmented generative models and robotics.
